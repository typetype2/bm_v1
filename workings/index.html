    HIDDEN

    The brief was to base a project around the word hidden.
    The project developed from research into old spy cameras that were hidden in all kinds of objects, from books to suitcases. Concealed out in the open, these pinhole cameras were made out of recycled materials, so they could be hidden in plain sight.
